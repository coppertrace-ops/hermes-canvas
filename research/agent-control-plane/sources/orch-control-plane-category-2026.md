## Key read for Hermes
The 2026 "agent control plane" gold rush is overwhelmingly **governance/security/identity/cost-first for FLEETS of enterprise agents** (who can run what, policy enforcement, cost attribution, registry/identity). It is NOT primarily about **human legibility of an individual run** (read the reasoning, see artifacts, approve/resume at a decision point). That distinction is the core whitespace argument for Hermes.
