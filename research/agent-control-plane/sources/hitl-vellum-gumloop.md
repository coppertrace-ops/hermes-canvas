## Vellum
Agent/LLM workflow orchestration + eval/observability platform. HITL positioned as default: "checkpoints can be set to wait for human approval before continuing"; "the assistant flags ambiguous cases, surfaces them in Slack or email, and waits for input." Pricing sales-led (no reliable self-serve number confirmed from primary page — third-party sources cite Free + Pro ~$50/mo and Business ~$79/user/mo; **treat as unverified**).
- HITL model: blocking approval checkpoint + eval gates. Dev-leaning (builder + eval + monitoring).
- Buyer: AI product/eng team. Deploy: SaaS.

## Gumloop
No-code AI-native automation platform (YC). "Gumloop allows agents to pause at important moments and check in with you for approval before taking action." Enterprise adoption cited (Shopify, Ramp, Gusto, Instacart, Opendoor). Raised $50M Series B.
- HITL model: blocking **human approval step** before external action; node-graph visual builder.
- Buyer: business/ops employees at AI-forward cos (non-dev). Deploy: SaaS. Pricing not captured verbatim here.

## Note
Both are workflow-builders where HITL is one node among many — NOT a dedicated agent-supervision/provenance console.
