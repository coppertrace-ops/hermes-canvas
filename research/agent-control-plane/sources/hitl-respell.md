## What it is
No-code AI workflow ("spells") builder with a Human Review step and "Respell Inbox." **Now part of Salesforce Agentforce** — third-party sources say the team joined Salesforce and the standalone platform is winding down (verify).

## Verbatim (per docs, via search)
- "The Human Review step allows you to send a configurable notification to your workspace that the output of a workflow should be reviewed."
- "A workspace user can... review the instructions, the approval outputs, and optionally input their own review before choosing Accept or Reject."
- "A spell is paused while it is being reviewed. If the review is Accepted, the spell will resume."

## HITL model
Blocking **inbox-based Accept/Reject** review, with optional reviewer input. Pause/resume run. Business-user oriented.

## Relevance
The **inbox + accept/reject + resume** pattern is close to Hermes conceptually — but Respell was absorbed by an enterprise CRM suite, i.e., the standalone HITL-inbox category consolidates into big platforms.
