## What it is
Workflow-automation platform explicitly positioned around "AI + human judgment working together." Multiple human-checkpoint types inside a visual builder; AI assistant runs on Claude.

## Verbatim (per site, via search)
- "Turn on human review for any AI step with a single toggle. Relay.app sends the output to you in email or Slack so you can approve, revise, or send it back."
- "Add an approval checkpoint anywhere in a workflow. Relay.app notifies the assignee and pauses the run until they approve."
- "Collect missing details with a small form... continues once it is filled out." (data-input HITL — close to Hermes "needs human input")

## HITL model
Blocking **approval checkpoint + review-any-AI-step + input-request form**. Pauses run until human acts. Business-user oriented; per-step review, not deep provenance/evidence trail.

## Buyer / persona / deploy / price
Business/ops teams (non-dev). SaaS. Free $0 (200 steps + 500 AI credits, 1 user); **Professional $19/mo**; **Team $69/mo** (up to 10 users).
